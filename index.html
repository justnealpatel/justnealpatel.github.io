About

I'm a recent graduate from Texas A&M University (Dec '18) and received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with minors in Cyber Security and Business Administration. I think what's kept me in this field thus far is because I've always loved to build things and being in this field allows me to continuously do that. I'm also a huge fan of open source. It helps to develop relationships between developers and allows the opportunity to create fantastic software. I made this website to be able to showcase some of the projects that I'm most proud of. Feel free to contact me below or connect with me through Linkedin.

The whole reason I got into software engineering is impact. I think that software engineering is one of the largest striving forces in creating change right now. For example, by publishing a mobile app, you can reach thousands of users and impact their lives. I think this is what got me interested. I would love to join a company whose mission speaks volumes in terms of change. I'm a results-driven, hard-working, and determined software engineer and if my background and mindset matches what you're looking for, don't hesitate to reach out here or on Linkedin. I have experience with Python (Flask, Django), iOS development (full stack), JS, HTML, CSS, Java, C++, MySQL, React, and Git.
